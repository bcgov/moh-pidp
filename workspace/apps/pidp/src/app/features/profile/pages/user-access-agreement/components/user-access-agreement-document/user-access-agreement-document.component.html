Access Harmonization User Access Agreement Background

This Agreement is made between you and His Majesty the King in right of the Province of British Columbia, as represented by the Minister of Health (the “Province”), and describes the basic terms and conditions you (the user) must comply with in order to enroll for access to clinical health systems available through the Province's Provider Identity Portal ("PIdP"), which may include but are not limited to an:

  • Electronic Medical Record (EMR),
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR),
  • Hospital Clinical Information System (CIS),
  • Health Information Banks (HIB),
  • or other electronic health data systems storing patient data in BC.

This Agreement does not guarantee access will be granted, System Owner(s) may, at their discretion, amend or change the scope of your access privileges to the System as privacy, security, business, and clinical practice requirements change. In such circumstances, the System Owner will notify you of such changes.

Definitions
  • "Provider" means a person who practices a designated health profession regulated by the Health Professions Act.
  • "Provider Identity Portal" means the Province's platform where Providers and Supervised users may apply for access to Systems.
  • "Supervised User" means any member of a Provider's staff who is required to access a System in relation to the Provider's practice.
  • "System" means the various provincial or private computerized information systems available to access health information in BC.
  • "System Owner" means the Health Authority, Ministry or organization responsible and accountable for data stored within a System.
User Access Agreement

For Providers registered with a Professional College in BC:


To only access the System(s) for so long as I am a registrant in good standing with my Professional College and my registration permits me to deliver direct patient care.

For Supervised Users Accessing System(s) on behalf of a College Registrant:

To only access the System(s) for so long as I am accessing the System(s) on behalf of a Provider that meets the criteria above.

I will only access the System(s) as necessary to perform my role and duties, unless permitted by the System Owner(s).

I will comply with the System Owner's breach management plan if I have knowledge of any circumstances, incidents or events which may jeopardize the security, confidentiality, availability, or integrity of the System(s) including any unauthorized attempt, by any person, to access the System(s).

I will not permit any other person to access the System(s) with my credentials and will take all reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access from occurring.

I will not provide any copies of records containing System(s) Data or "print outs" produced directly from the System(s) and will refer any requests for access to such records to the System(s) Owner.

I will store all relevant System(s) data in accordance with my or my supervising Provider's Professional College Requirements and the System Owner's organizational policies.

I will ensure that any personal information I record in the System(s) is accurate, complete, and up to date.

Where I am aware of any inaccuracy or error in such information, I will take action to investigate and correct it. I will notify the System(s) Owner of the inaccuracy/error and any steps taken.

I will cooperate with any audits or investigations conducted by the System(s) Owner regarding compliance with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FOIPPA), the E-Health (Personal Health Information Access and Protection of Privacy) Act, any other applicable statute, and this Agreement. This may include providing access upon request to your facilities, data management systems, books, records, and personnel for the purposes of auditing or investigation.

I acknowledge the System(s) Owner may report any material breach of this Agreement to my Professional College, the Office of the Information and Privacy Commissioner of British Columbia, and/or my organization/supervising Provider.

The terms and conditions of this Agreement may be amended by the Province at any time, in its sole discretion, by the Province delivering written notice of the amendment to the email address indicated in your PIdP profile. Any such notice will specify the date upon which the amendment becomes effective. If you continue to access any of the Systems enrolled through the PIDP after the effective date of the amendment, you will be deemed to have accepted the amendment. If you do not agree with an amendment for which notice has been provided by the Province, you must promptly (and in any event prior to the effective date) cease all access or use of all Systems enrolled through the PIDP.
