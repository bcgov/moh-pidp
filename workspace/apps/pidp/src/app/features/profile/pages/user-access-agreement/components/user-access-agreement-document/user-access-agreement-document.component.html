Access Harmonization User Access Agreement Background

This Agreement describes the basic terms and conditionsd for you must comply with in order to gain access to clinical health systems available through the Provider Identity Portal (PIdP), which may include but are not limited to an:

  • Electronic Medical Record (EMR),
  • Electronic Health Record (EHR),
  • Hospital Clinical Information System (CIS),
  • Health Information Banks (HIB),
  • or other electronic health data systems storing patient data in BC.

For full legal terms, consult the Terms of Service for each respective system available in your PIdP Profile. System Owner(s) may, at their discretion, amend or change the scope of your access privileges to the System as privacy, security, business, and clinical practice requirements change. In such circumstances, the System Owner will notify you of such changes.

Definitions
  • "Provider" means a person who practices a designated health professional as regulated by the Health Professions Act.
  • "Provider Identity Portal" means the BC Ministry of Health platform where Providers and Supervised users may apply for access to Systems.
  • "Supervised User" means any member of a Provider's staff who is required to access a System in relation to the Provider's practice.
  • "System" means the various provincial or private computerized information systems available to access health information in BC.
  • "System Owner" means the Health Authority, Ministry or organization responsible and accountable for data stored within a System
User Access Agreement

For Providers registered with a Professional College in BC:


To only access the System(s) for so long as I am a registrant in good standing with my Professional College and my registration permits me to deliver direct patient care. For Supervised Users Accessing System(s) on behalf of a College Registrant: To only access the System(s) for so long as I am accessing the System(s) on behalf of a Provider that meets the criteria above.

I will only access the System(s) as necessary to perform my role and duties in the provision of direct patient care, unless permitted by the System Owner(s) and in accordance with an information sharing plan or agreement.

I will comply with my organization's breach management plan if I have knowledge of any circumstances, incidents or events which may jeopardize the security, confidentiality, availability, or integrity of the System(s) including any unauthorized attempt, by any person, to access the System(s).

I will not permit any other person to access the System(s) with my credentials, and will take all reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access from occurring.

I will only access the System(s) at the sites permitted, unless compliant with System(s) Owner remote access policies, and/or with explicit approval from the System(s) Owner.

I will not provide patients with any copies of records containing System(s) Data or "print outs" produced directly from the System(s), and will refer any requests for access to such records to the System(s) Owner.

I will store all relevant System(s) data in accordance with my or my supervising Provider's Professional College Requirements and my organizational policies

I will ensure that any Personal Information I record in the System(s) is accurate, complete, and up to date.

Where I am aware of any inaccuracy or error in such information, I will take action to investigate and, correct it. I will notify the System(s) Owner of the inaccuracy/error and any steps taken.

I will cooperate with any audits or investigations conducted by the System(s) Owner regarding compliance with the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FOIPPA), the eHealth Act and this Agreement. This may include providing access upon request to your facilities, data management systems, books, records and personnel for the purposes of auditing or investigation.

I acknowledge the System(s) Owner may report any material breach of this Agreement to my Professional College, the Information and Privacy Commissioner of British Columbia, and/or my organization/supervising Provider.

Any notice(s) to the User related to the terms of this Agreement will be provided in writing to the User's email address entered into the Provider Identity Portal "Demographic Information" section
